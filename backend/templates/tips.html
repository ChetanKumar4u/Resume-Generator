Resume Writing Tips

Create a compelling resume that stands out and effectively showcases your professional journey.

Format and Structure

  • Keep your resume to 1-2 pages maximum
  • Use clear, professional fonts (Arial, Calibri, or similar)
  • Maintain consistent formatting throughout
  • Include clear section headings
  • Use bullet points for better readability
  • Ensure adequate white space

Content Writing

  • Use action verbs to begin bullet points
  • Quantify achievements when possible
  • Tailor content to the job description
  • Focus on relevant experience
  • Proofread thoroughly for errors
  • Keep descriptions concise and impactful

Essential Sections

  • Contact information at the top
  • Professional summary or objective
  • Work experience in reverse chronological order
  • Education and certifications
  • Relevant skills and competencies
  • Optional: achievements, volunteer work, projects

ATS Optimization

  • Use standard section headings
  • Include keywords from the job description
  • Avoid tables and complex formatting
  • Use standard file formats (PDF, DOCX)
  • Spell out acronyms at least once
  • Keep the design simple and readable

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Including personal information (age, marital status)
  • Using generic objectives
  • Including irrelevant experience
  • Making spelling or grammar errors
  • Using an unprofessional email address
  • Including references on the resume

Professional Branding

  • Maintain a consistent personal brand
  • Link to relevant professional profiles
  • Highlight unique value proposition
  • Include industry-specific keywords
  • Show career progression
  • Emphasize relevant achievements
