Resume Writing Tips

Expert advice to help you create a compelling resume that stands out.

Formatting

Keep It Clean and Consistent

Use consistent formatting throughout your resume. Stick to one or two professional fonts and maintain consistent spacing and alignment.

Content

Highlight Achievements

Focus on quantifiable achievements rather than just listing job duties. Use numbers and metrics to demonstrate your impact.

Strategy

Tailor Your Resume

Customize your resume for each job application. Match your skills and experiences to the job requirements.
